Tezos
	"Tezos attempts to represent a blockchain protocol in the most general way possible while remaining as efficient as a native protocol"

	 - modular, designed to enable drop in replacement of most components without hard fork
	 - has a built in governance mechanism
	 - uses a functional scripting language

	"Tezos solves a tragedy of the commons problem by providing a way to facilitate protocol dynamism through on-chain governance mechanism"
